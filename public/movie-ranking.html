Rue's Amazing Movie List

Top 3 Movies of All Time!

Labyrinth

I love this movie. It combines fantasy with coming of age.

Synopsis

A teenage girl ventures into a mystical maze to rescue her baby brother from the clutches of the Goblin King and must navigate a fantastical world filled with riddles and strange creatures to succeed.

The Princess Bride

The characters in this movie are just so likeable.

Synopsis

Tells the enchanting tale of a young woman and her true love as they overcome numerous obstacles, including pirates, giants, and an evil prince, to be reunited and live happily ever after.

Pan's Labyrinth

Beautiful movie with an intriguing storyline.

Synopsis

Pan's Labyrinth weaves a dark and mesmerizing story set in post-Civil War Spain, following a young girl who escapes into a mythical labyrinth where she must complete dangerous tasks to prove her worthiness and fulfill her destiny as a princess of an ancient underworld realm.
